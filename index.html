Hit-Sort

My sorting algorithm can be used for big positive integer arrays only, 12 times faster than bubble sort.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

We come across situations in real life where we may have to sort positive integers because that's how we count in real life, but when the count becomes big and basic sorts won't work we need something more than just comparing two numbers. This is sorting algorithm to make your job easier. At 10,000 numbers in an array it takes less than 1/5th of a second to sort and his 12 times faster than regular basic sorts. When the numbers in the array get more and more it becomes harder and harder to solve with regular sorts, but this will sort the array in much smaller time. When tested with 10000000 numbers it did the job without throwing maximum depth error in a much faster time. I will make the same code in all the languages I'm familiar with
